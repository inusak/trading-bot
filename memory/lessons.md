# Trading Lessons (append-only, prompt-cached)

## Day 0 — Priors

- SPY buy-and-hold is the benchmark. Underperforming it makes the strategy a loss.
- Max 3 orders per day. Every order must clear a quality bar, not fill budget.
- Momentum > prediction. Trade continuation of established moves on strong volume.
- RSI > 75 = overbought caution. RSI < 25 = oversold, possible bounce but confirm volume.
- Avoid fresh long entries in last 15 min. Preclose slot is for risk management.
- Never average down losers without a new thesis. Cutting losses fast beats hoping.
- Position size cap: 15% of portfolio per order.
